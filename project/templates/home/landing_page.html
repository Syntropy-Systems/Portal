About

WePro BPO has been established as a Broker Price Opinion outsourcing company which caters all types of Residential and Commercial properties throughout United States.

Our company started as a small business partnership in 2008.
With the perseverance, commitment and continuously delivering high quality reports of the people behind, the business started to grow and created a team of highly skilled real estate analyst who are best experienced in processing and formulating standard quality, error-free, quick turn around time and cost effective broker price opinions.

Services

  • Providing the County Public Record of a property
  • Pulling Comparable using Multiple Listing System
  • Data Entry on Valuation Form.
  • Uploading Comparable Photos
  • 24/7 monitoring and support to client's issue
  • Rush/Normal order processing
    • Rush order - 6 hours' time frame
    • normal order - 24 hours' time frame
  • QC Issue responded within 6 hours' time frame

Comp Searches

Multiple Listing System is the main source of our data. By providing three sold listing and three active listings and Setting criteria for proximity, GLA, age, style and room count we identify the subject's current market value.

Data Entry

All fields in the form are inputted, from subject property, neighborhood up to the comparable with unique value conclusion comment for each property. Adjustment also added to determine the best current value of the subject.

Attachment

All necessary source of information that has been used in the valuation analysis will be uploaded in the form such as subject property tax record and/or Original appraisal, Subject MLS sheet (if the subject property has a pending offer). Comparable MLS sheets also uploaded if needed in the valuation platform.
